Why Practera Employ

  • Hands-On Projects

    Real Experience with Real Projects

    Many training programs offer a lot of content, but not enough practice. With Practera Employ, skills are developed through hands-on projects that mimic real-world situations. There is a strong focus on teamwork, communication and leadership skills, which are highly desired by employers.

  • Job Placement & Retention

    Feedback from Industry Experts. A Network of Opportunity.

    To ensure our learners can be quickly placed into jobs, we leverage our own global network of 1000+ employers. Our post-hire job coaching program will boost retention by providing opportunities for advice and feedback on the new job from an expert during their first 90 days.

  • Zero-Risk & Accessible

    Practera Employ is Free for Learners.

    Practera Employ is free for learners and can be done at their own pace and at times that suit their work and life requirements. Build confidence in your new abilities with real projects. Provide real-world examples that can be used in interviews.
